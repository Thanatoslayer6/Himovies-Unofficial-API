Himovies-Unofficial-API

Search for a tv-show or a movie: /search/[query]

### MOVIES ###

Get Movie Servers: /movie/[movieId]

### TV-SHOW ###

Get TV Seasons: /tv/seasons/[tvId]

Get TV Episodes: /tv/episodes/[seasonId]

Get TV Episode servers: /tv/servers/[episodeId]

### LINKS ###

Get TV Episode or Movie links: /links/[serverId]?href=[episodeId or movieId]
